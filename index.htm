Life Cover

Life Cover is one of the cheapest and safest form of insurance cover available. Of course this comes with a warning, as a life company will asses your risk of dying before insuring you, and in some cases this may prove to be expensive or you are unable to get insurance.

You should cover all your long term debts using life cover, if you have dependants. by this, we mean, if you die, will those left behind be financially worse off. If yes, cover you debts at a minimume.

Lump sum disability cover

The main thing to keep in mind when buying this type of cover, is what you are covered for. Disability cover is depedant on what you do for a living and if you can continue to earn from your job in the event of a disability. This cover is good, however you must ensure that you get the right cover.

Some questions you should ask your broker, or the person trying to sell you this cover :

  • Am i covered for my own occupation?
  • What events will I be paid out? (think fo practical examples that pertain to you)
  • Will I get partial payment if I can continue to work in a diminshed capacity
